# 🍎 Apple Product Pricing & Market Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Analytics case study that analyzes Apple product pricing across multiple e-commerce platforms between 2020 and 2026.

The objective is to identify pricing trends, evaluate discount strategies, analyze customer satisfaction, and generate business recommendations using Python.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Apple products are sold across multiple online platforms with varying pricing strategies, promotional discounts, and customer reviews.

The business wants to understand:

- Which platforms offer the highest discounts?
- Which product categories maintain premium pricing?
- Which products retain their value the best?
- Which promotional events provide the highest discounts?
- How do customer ratings differ across categories and platforms?

---

## 📊 Dataset Information

- Records: ~80,000+
- Time Period: 2020–2026
- Categories: iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch
- Platforms: Multiple online retailers
